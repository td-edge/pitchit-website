The Game

Four steps to glory
(or glorious failure)

Each day brings a new sound. You get three attempts to nail it, and your worst scores are often the funniest to share.

🔊

Step 01

Hear the sound

Each day features a new challenge: a dog bark, a car engine, a thunderclap. Listen to the original as many times as you like.

🎤

Step 02

Record your take

Hit record and give it your best impression. You get three attempts per day. Use them wisely, or just go completely unhinged.

📊

Step 03

Get your score

Our algorithm compares your recording using pitch, timbre, and timing. Scores range from 0 to 100. A 74 means you're good. A 12 means you're comedy gold.

🚀

Step 04

Share & climb

Post your score to TikTok, stories, or anywhere. Challenge friends. Hit the weekly leaderboard. Low scores are comedy. Share those too.
